how to properly make a egg breakfast

  • First: Wash mushrooms and cut in to nice even pieces, add seasoning per prefrence
  • Second: Cut tomatos in even pieces, add salt and pepper per choice
  • Third: Take sausages and cut in to wished thickness
  • Fourth: Add mushrooms and sausages to the pre heated pan and stir until flavour and colour comes out
  • Fifth: place eggs carefully on to pan and smoosh to getter to create a threeheaded chicken baby
  • Sixth: Let the tomatos enjoy the fun and place in pan, continue to cook until firealarm turn on
